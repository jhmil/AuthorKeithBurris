Duo Destiny:

Bounty Hunter:

Ryan Singleton

Bounty Hunter Series

Ryan Singleton, a spirited and determined young man, found himself at a crossroads in life. The monotony of working under his father's watchful eye had grown wearisome, and a strong desire to chase his own aspirations ignited within him. His heart yearned to become a bounty hunter—a figure of grit and adventure, unburdened by the ordinary. However, the path he embarked upon was anything but a walk in the park. The world of bounty hunting unveiled its challenges like a rugged terrain—full of unexpected twists and daunting hurdles. The thrill he had envisioned was tempered by the harsh reality of the task at hand. Fortune smiled upon him when Jody Winters, a revered and battle-tested bounty hunter, extended a helping hand. She recognized the fire within Ryan and saw the potential that was waiting to be honed. Under her seasoned tutelage, he learned the ropes of the trade—the art of tracking, the dance of deception, and the psychology of capturing those who thought themselves beyond reach. Ryan Singleton's journey showcases the power of an unyielding spirit. He defied the expectations that life had placed upon him and embraced the chaotic unknown with arms wide open. Guided by his strong moral compass, he set out to not only capture fugitives but also to capture the essence of his own destiny—a destiny where he would not just follow the path, but carve it with his own footsteps.
